About Us

We are a group of people reducing the gap between the company`s demand and academic world to increase the productivity of students.

There is a gap between companies and students in terms of the hiring process. Companies want to hire new people and increase their productivity while students want to get hired and work but due to the gap they both are not able to achieve their goals. Companies have to settle for a subpar employee while students remain underpaid or even unemployed. We as a group of people feel that this gap should be filled.

“Campus Amigo” is a team which prepares the students for the tough fight towards employment while moulding them into worthy candidates who are interview ready. We teach and emphasize on production-ready tools and paradigms while improving their interview preparation and taking their interview fear away.
